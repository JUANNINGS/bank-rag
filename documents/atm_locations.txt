## ATM Safety and Security



### Safety Tips

- Use ATMs in well-lit, busy areas when possible

- Be aware of your surroundings

- Shield the keypad when entering your PIN

- Don't count cash in public

- Cancel the transaction if ATM seems tampered with or has unusual attachments

- Never accept "help" from strangers at ATMs



### Reporting Issues



**ATM out of service or card retained:**

- Call 13 MELB (13 6352) immediately - available 24/7

- Use in-app chat support

- Visit nearest branch during business hours



**Suspected card skimming or fraud:**

- Do not use the ATM

- Call 13 MELB immediately

- Report location details

- Check your account for unauthorized transactions

## Special Features



### Cardless Cash

Access cash without your physical card using the Melbourne First Bank mobile app:

1. Open mobile app and select "Cardless Cash"

2. Choose withdrawal amount ($20-$500)

3. Receive secure 6-digit code

4. Go to any Melbourne First Bank ATM within 3 hours

5. Select "Cardless Withdrawal"

6. Enter code and collect cash



**Available at all Melbourne First Bank ATMs**



### Deposit Cheques at ATMs

Many Melbourne First Bank ATMs accept cheque deposits:

- Insert cheque (no envelope required)

- ATM scans and images the cheque

- Funds typically available next business day

- Cheques deposited after 5 PM available in 2 business days

- Maximum 30 cheques per deposit



### Accessibility Features

All Melbourne First Bank ATMs feature:

- Audio guidance for vision-impaired customers

- Headphone jack (standard 3.5mm)

- Braille keypads and instructions

- Raised tactile markings

- Adjustable screen height at accessible ATMs

- Wheelchair access at all branch ATMs
